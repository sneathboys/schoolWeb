Bedwars


Bedwars is a mini game on the Hypixel network. The aim of the game is to destroy everyones beds to stop them respawning and kill them to win the game. However, you also have to protect your own bed as you do not want it destroyed, you can surround the bed with blocks each with a different difucilty to mine but at increaring costs.


Currency


Iron

Iron is a common resource that is used to buy many basic matirials souch as blocks, stone sword and chain armour. These items are used throughout gameplay and are easly obtained due to the common production of iron. It can be collected from the generator.



Gold

Gold is a valuable resource that spawns rarly from the genorator, it can be used to buy many valubale items such as tnt, golden apples, iron armour and more powerful tools aswell as wepons. These are slow to obtain but are very powerful as they give a many different helpful abilities. All matirials can be obtained by killing players who have them in their inventories.



Diamonds

Diamonds is highly valuable resource that allows you to buy enchantments and other stat increases for your gear. This allows you to be able to take less damage, do more damage and heal on your island faster. Diamonds can be obtained via moving to the small islands next to your bed and sitting at the genorator. People will fight over access to these diamonds.



Emeralds

Emeralds are the rarest most valubale currency in bedwars, they are in the middle of thge map and are highly contested as their is only two genorators that produce one emerald every 30 seconds. These can be used to buy overpowered potions or the bewst gear and tools. THis allows any user with access to them to easily defeat compatition.
